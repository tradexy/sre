About Me, Cloud Engineer with AWS and Azure work experience


Perfect for Cloud Engineering

With Passion For Cloud Development

The key task for SREs is automating. SREs reduce toil, meaning manual work that doesn't add anything to the business and just causes problems.
SREs are absolute automation machines. You can see how creativity, collaboration, communication, and various technical skills fit in.
Let's face it, your job in operations doesn't lend itself to creativity. Operations is very reactive. You do the same work every day—chasing after and fixing problems.
The SRE role, on the other hand, allows you to affect both the future of your organization and the customer experience. From a career perspective, the job of an SRE is much more rewarding than most IT Ops positions, because you can use your abilities to create, design, improve, and re-engineer.
Essentially, an SRE replaces human labor with automation, generally by creating self-service tools for developers. An SRE team enhances the availability, performance, efficiency, monitoring, emergency response, and planning of production services and software.
